# Customer Strategic Value Analysis & Portfolio Optimization

## Overview
This project analyzes customer behavior for an e-commerce platform (Equinox) to identify high-value customers and design a data-driven investment strategy to improve long-term customer portfolio quality.

A **Customer Strategic Value Framework** was developed to evaluate customers across economic contribution, engagement depth, stability risk, and growth potential. Customers were segmented and strategically allocated into **CORE (stable revenue drivers)** and **APEX (high-growth potential)** groups.

---

## Objectives
- Build a **Customer Strategic Value scoring framework**
- Segment customers based on strategic importance
- Allocate customers between **CORE** and **APEX** teams
- Design a **₹1 Crore capital allocation strategy**
- Estimate the impact on overall **customer portfolio quality**

---

## Key Results
- Identified that **~37% of customers drive strategic value** (CORE + APEX).
- Designed targeted initiatives to improve premium adoption, conversion, and engagement.
- Simulated improvements showing a **~13% increase in Customer Strategic Value**.

---

## Workbook Guide (Excel Color Coding)
To improve readability of the Excel analysis model:

- **Yellow:** Original dataset variables (e.g., monthly spend, premium subscription)
- **Orange:** Transformed metrics such as normalized values, ranks, and percentile ranks
- **Red:** Calculated framework components (Economic, Engagement, Stability, Growth scores)
- **Blue:** Final analytical outputs including **Customer Strategic Value Score, Customer Segment, and Team Allocation**
